Frequently Asked Questions

Please visit the “About the Station” page to learn more about space accommodations.

Do I need training?
Only for zero gravity activities.
What medical conditions prevent me from participating?
Heart problems (and certain other serious cardiovascular or respiratory conditions. Full medical screening required during booking).
Are there age restrictions?
Minimum age to ride in the rocket to the station is 5 years old.
Is vaping or smoking allowed?
No, vaping and smoking are strictly prohibited onboard for safety, air quality, and environmental control system reasons.
